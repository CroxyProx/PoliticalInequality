Bartels, L. M. (2008). Economic inequality and political representation. Russell Sage Foundation.
Shows how politicians often respond more to wealthy constituents than to average voters.
Cagé, J. (2024). Political inequality. Annual Review of Economics, 16, 455–490. https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev-economics-080223-040921

Argues that rising inequality can weaken democratic institutions over time.
